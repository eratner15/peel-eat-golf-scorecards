Selection Protocol Key

  • P: Partner Selected (2v2 format)
  • L: Lone Wolf (Wolf vs All Others)
  • B: Blind Selection (Partner pre-tee)
  • BL: Blind Lone Wolf (Solo pre-tee)

How to Play Wolf

  1. Players rotate being Wolf in sequence
  2. Wolf selects partner (P) or goes solo (L)
  3. Team with lowest score on hole wins points
  4. Blind selections (B/BL) before tee shots

Point Structure

  • 2v2 Win: Wolf=2pts, Partner=1pt
  • 2v2 Loss: Opponents=2pts each
  • 1v3 Win: Wolf=4pts
  • 1v3 Loss: Opponents=1pt each
  • Blind: 2X points
  • Blind Lone Wolf: 3X points

Skins Game Rules

  • Lowest score on each hole wins the "skin."
  • Ties result in the skin's value carrying over (C/O) to the next hole.
  • All carryovers accumulate until one player wins a hole outright.
  • Skins can be valued at a fixed rate or increase progressively.
  • Optional Rules (Confirm with group):
  • Validation: Player may need to validate a skin win (e.g., with par or better on the next hole).
  • Progressive Pot: Increase the value of skins as the round progresses.
